## History

### Origins (1877–1879)

- Informal predecessor operated 1877–1879 under the colourful name **"Petoeläinten hävittämisosakeyhtiö"** (Predator Extermination Corporation).
- Officially founded in **spring 1879** as **Tampereen Metsästys- ja Ampumaseura** (Tampere Hunting and Shooting Club), when permits were obtained from the Imperial Senate of Finland and the city magistrate.
- Later renamed to **Pohjois-Hämeen Ampujat**.

### Early Years

- First shooting range was at **Pyynikki**, on the shores of Lake Pyhäjärvi. Last competitions there in summer 1900, after which the club relocated to **Kulju** south of Tampere due to noise complaints from summer residents.
- In its early years the club focused heavily on **hunting matters** — acting as a guiding, informing, and supervisory expert on all hunting-related issues around Tampere. It paid bounties for hawks, owls, crows, and their eggs, and compiled information about predators.
- Early competitions used feather-filled glass balls launched into the air, which later evolved into **Olympic trap shooting**.
- The club has operated across **three centuries** (19th, 20th, and 21st).

### Notable Historical Figure

- **Karl Frans Hjorth (1857–1919):** Enthusiastic member and skilled shooter. Originally a general goods merchant, his passion for shooting led him into the firearms and ammunition trade. The Tampere-based **Hjorth** is noted as Finland's oldest firearms business.

### Key Milestones

- **1894:** First inter-club competition (lost to Helsinki).
- **1999:** Kokkovuori hosted the **Double Trap World Championship** — won by Pia Nybäck.
- **2009:** 130th anniversary celebrated at Kokkovuori, Lempäälä.
- **2019:** 140th anniversary — festive dinner with speeches, awards, entertainment.
- **2023:** Kokkovuori hosted shotgun events for the **European Masters Games (EMG 2023)** in Tampere.
- **125-year anniversary book:** "Susijahdista kilpa-ammuntaan — Pohjois-Hämeen Ampujat 125 vuotta" by Ari Järvelä (2006), ISBN 952-91-9933-3.

### Observations for Redesign

- **Strengths:** Contains comprehensive information; structured by club section; calendar and news functionality.
- **Weaknesses:** Generic template platform (Sporttisaitti); limited visual appeal; no hero imagery or modern landing experience; social media links not prominent; no English content; the Kokkovuori shotgun section has already moved to its own separate site (kokkovuoren.fi), fragmenting the club's online presence.
- **Opportunity:** A modern, visually striking single-page or multi-page demo could showcase the club's rich 145+ year heritage, world-class facilities, diverse disciplines, and welcoming community. The club's history spanning three centuries is a unique selling point. Strong photography assets exist (ranges, competitions, flags, logos).

## Design Inspiration Analysis — International Shooting Sport Sites

Two national-level shooting sport federation websites were analyzed for design patterns applicable to PHA's new site.

### USA Shooting (usashooting.org)

**Overall feel:** Dark, clean, professional, athletic. Achieves its sporty identity primarily through typography and photography, not complex animations.

**Key design patterns:**

| Element           | Implementation                                                                                                                                                           |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Color palette** | Charcoal `#28303D` + white + single red accent (CTAs only). Deliberately restrained.                                                                                     |
| **Typography**    | Condensed italic uppercase headings ("Blunt-ConIt") with `letter-spacing: 2px` — the single biggest contributor to the "sporty" feel. Body: clean sans-serif at 18–20px. |
| **Hero**          | Full-bleed athlete carousel (6 slides, 5s auto-advance, 800ms transition). Dark overlay for text legibility. Centered white text + pill-shaped CTA.                      |
| **Navigation**    | Two-tier: utility bar (Login, Find Club, Contact, Search, Join) + primary nav (logo left, links right). Sticky, transparent-to-solid on scroll.                          |
| **News cards**    | 3-column grid. Text-forward: date \| category, headline, excerpt, "More" link. No thumbnails in listings.                                                                |
| **Events**        | Interactive monthly calendar (Tribe Events plugin). List/Month/Day views. Calendar subscription export (Google, iCal, Outlook).                                          |
| **Membership**    | 3-column tier cards (Annual, 5-Year, Lifetime). Image + description + red "JOIN NOW" button each.                                                                        |
| **Partners**      | 40+ logos in 3-per-row grid (28% width). Max-width 400px, min-width 150px per logo.                                                                                      |
| **Footer**        | 4-column link grid (Help, Resources, About, Get Involved) + address + social icons + newsletter signup.                                                                  |
| **Spacing**       | Container padding: 70px desktop / 40px mobile. Section margins: 40–50px. Generous whitespace throughout.                                                                 |
| **Mobile**        | Primary breakpoint 800px. Cards stack single-column. Padding drops to ~11px 5px on small screens.                                                                        |

**What makes it feel sporty:**

1. Condensed italic uppercase headings — creates speed and dynamism
2. Full-bleed athlete photography with dark overlays
3. Action-oriented CTA language ("Join Today", "Get Shooting")
4. Minimal decoration — no gradients, no ornamental elements
5. High contrast (dark + white + one accent color)
6. Stats/numbers displayed in large bold type

### Synthesized Design Direction for PHA

Based on both inspiration sites, here is the recommended design system:

#### Color System

| Role             | Recommended                 | Notes                                          |
| ---------------- | --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Primary dark     | `#0a1621` (deep navy)       | Headers, footers, hero overlays, dark sections |
| Background light | `#F0F5FA` or `#f8f9fa`      | Alternating light sections                     |
| White            | `#FFFFFF`                   | Cards, text on dark                            |
| Accent (CTA)     | PHA brand color (from logo) | Join/CTA buttons only — use sparingly          |
| Body text        | `#28303D`                   | Readable near-black                            |
| Subtle           | `rgba(0,0,0,0.1)`           | Borders, shadows                               |

#### Typography

- **Headings:** Bold condensed sans-serif, uppercase, letter-spaced. Free alternatives: Barlow Condensed, Oswald, or Bebas Neue.
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif — Inter, Poppins, or system stack.
- **Scale:** Hero 56px → H1 36px → H2 28px → H3 22px → Body 18px.

#### Layout Patterns

- **Hero:** Full-bleed photo carousel with dark overlay + white text + pill CTA
- **Navigation:** Sticky two-tier or single-tier dark header. Logo left, "Liity jäseneksi" CTA button right.
- **Content sections:** Alternating dark/light backgrounds. Max-width 1280px container.
- **Cards:** 3-column grid (news, events, disciplines). Subtle shadow + hover zoom on images.
- **Sponsors:** Logo grid, 3–4 columns, consistent sizing.
- **Footer:** 4-column dark link grid + social icons + partner logos + legal bar.

#### Key Interactions

- Hero carousel auto-advance (5s) with smooth transitions
- Image hover zoom (`scale(1.05)`, 0.3s ease)
- Button color transitions (0.2s)
- Sticky nav: transparent → solid on scroll
- Smooth scroll for anchor links
- Subtle scroll-triggered fade-in for content sections (optional)

#### Mobile Strategy

- Primary breakpoint: 768px
- Cards stack to single column
- Full-width CTA buttons
- Hamburger menu with slide-in drawer
- Hero height reduces to 380px
- Section padding: 70px → 20px
- Min touch target: 44px
